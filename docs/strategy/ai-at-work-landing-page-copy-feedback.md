## Value Props

Section eyebrow:
why a check-in?

Section title:
The part every AI rollout skips

### An honest conversation, not a survey

People won't put their real AI fears in a feedback form. They'll say them out loud when it's safe
to. The check-in creates that room — and gets the quiet stuff on the table.

### About 20 minutes. That's the whole ask.

No workshop, no consultant, no slide deck to prep. Draw a card, ask the question, listen. One team
meeting is all it takes to start.

### Free, and built for real teams

Works in person or remote, across languages and time zones. No login, no install, nothing to buy.
Just open the deck and begin.
